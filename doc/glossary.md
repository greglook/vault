Glossary

| Term       | Symbols | Types                        | Definition |
|------------|:-------:|------------------------------|------------|
| hash-id    | `id`    | `vault.blob.digest.HashID`   | A hash identifier for a blob of data. |

| blob store | `store` | `vault.blob.store.BlobStore` | A system which stores blob data. |
